  \item Objective :
  \begin{itemize}
    \item Identifying the average relative interactions between the pairs of drops colliding.

$P_{d_{ndr}}(d_{ndr}) = \iint P(\bm{\lambda}) du_{rel}d\theta d\beta.$\hfill
